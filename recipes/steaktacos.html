Steak Tacos

These delicious street style tacos are made with seared steak bites and pickled red onions.

Yummo!

Ingredients

For Tacos

  • Top Sirloin Steak
  • Pickled Red Onions
  • Cilantro
  • Lime
  • Flour tortillas, Sommer!
  • free shavacado

For Seasoning

  • Paprika
  • Cumin
  • Garlic Powder
  • Chilli Powder

Instructions

  1. In a small bowl combine 1/3 tbsp of paprika, a tsp of cumin, 1 tsp of chilli power and a tsp of garlic powder for your seasoning
  2. Preheat the oven to 200 to warm tortillas
  3. Prepare steak by trimming excess fat and cubing into 1/2 inch blocks
  4. Season the cubed steak evenly, let sit for 10 minutes
  5. Begin dicing cilantro and avacado
  6. Heat the tortillas in the oven while preparing meat
  7. Add some oil to a skillet and warm on high heat
  8. without crowding the pan, sear the steak bites on high for 1 minute, then flip for 1 minute. Then stir in the pan for about a minute until fully browned
  9. Set the meat aside to rest
  10. Remove the tortillas from the oven
  11. Bring all the prepared ingredients to the table in bowls, assemble your street taco with your prefferred toppings and enjoy
