Creamy Cottage Cheese Marinara

Delicious, nutritious and hopefully done before she gets hangry

Ingredients:

  • Dry pasta of choice
  • Olive oil
  • yellow onion
  • garlic cloves
  • ground meat
  • cottage cheese
  • salt n pepper
  • fresh basil
  • parmeezian cheese

Instructions

  1. Start the pasta cooking
  2. heat the oil and saute the onion
  3. season the saute
  4. add minced garlic, saute
  5. add ground meat
  6. blend the cheese and marinara until creamy
  7. combine pasta sauce and cream, let rest

Now enjoy you rats
