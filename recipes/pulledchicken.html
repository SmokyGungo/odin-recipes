Pulled Chicken and Rice Bowls

A tasty dish that you can set and forget in the slow cooker, or go out for drinks and let the beautiful Sommer finish it up on your behalf

Ingredients

  • Chicken Thighs
  • Apple Cider Vinegar
  • Soy Sauce
  • Honey
  • Garlic
  • Tomato Paste
  • Black Pepper
  • Red Pepper Flakes
  • Corn Starch
  • White Rice

Instructions

  1. Mix the a 1/3 cup of honey, vinegar, soy sauce, 4 cloves of finely diced garlic and the tomato paste together

  2. Place the chicken into a slow cookers and pour the sauce over it
  3. Let cook on high for 2-3 hours or low for 5-8 hours
  4. Remove the chicken and set aside
  5. In a bowl mix 1/2 cup of water and 1/2 cup of cornstarch and mix to paste
  6. add the cornstarch paste to the sauce in the slow cooker, let cook for 30 mins, stirring often
  7. Shred the cooked chicken and add back into thickened sauce, mix it in
  8. Cook some rice on the side and pour slow cooked chicken over rice bowl, garnish as you please
